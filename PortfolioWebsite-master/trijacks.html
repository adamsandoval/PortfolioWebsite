Trijacks

Challange

The NAU Trijacks is the club triathlon team at Northern Arizona University, my Alma Mater. As an active member of the Trijacks during my four years there and as the only graphic designer I took it upon myself to create the NAU Trijacks logo and marketing items. It was important that the logo reflects both the essence of the sport as well as the city where NAU is located, Flagstaff Arizona. The hatch technique creates a certain depth and also gives the logo a sense of movement. I incorporated a mountain banner to represent the mountain town that is Flagstaff. The icons (water drop, bike chain link, and foot) represent the three disciplines of a triathlon: swimming, biking, and running. The biggest challenge I met was choosing three icons that would both represent their individual disciplines and whose forms would also complement each other in the design.
